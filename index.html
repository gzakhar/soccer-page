Croton Soccer League

Post-college pickup soccer for anyone 18 and over — no tryouts, no season-long commitment. Just show up and play.

18 & over Post-college Sundays & Wednesdays Spencer Field

What it is

We're a group of players who wanted to keep playing after college — so we started running games at Spencer Field. The soccer is real and competitive, but the vibe stays friendly. Teams get sorted on the spot, everyone plays, and the goal is a good run with good people.

No sign-up, no dues, no experience level required. Bring cleats and both a light and a dark shirt, get there a few minutes early, and someone will get you on a side.
